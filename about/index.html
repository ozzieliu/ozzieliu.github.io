About Ozzie

    I am Oz, the Great and Powerful!
    Who are you? And why do you seek me?

        - "The Wizard of Oz"

OK, I'm not a wizard that can give you a new heart, brain, or courage (yet), but behind the curtain, I am a data scientist passionate about exploring the heart, the insights, and the guts of data.

I use data to explore big questions and tell compelling stories.

Currently

I am a Senior Data Analyst at Casper's Marketing Analytics team. The work I do is, by all means data science, we just don't call it that because our team's primary goal is to provide quick and actionable insights for the business - whether that requires machine learning, or just a quick chart in Excel.

Previously, I was at MLB Advanced Media's Advanced Analytics team working as consultants for all 30 MLB teams' front offices with lead scoring, churn prediction, customer segmentation, geographical analyses, attendance and ticket sales projections. We also supported MLBAM's experiential apps like MLB At Bat, BAM's streaming platform and its partners with customer lifetime value, churn modeling, email and marketing campaigns.

I also attended Metis Data Science Bootcamp to brush up and prepare myself for a career in data science. My goal is to formally work on interesting data science projects with machine learning, data mining, data visualization, and web development to create cool data science products.

Experience

Before this, I've worked with data in many different ways. I interned as a data scientist for an insurance company and I helped bring data analytics to the Big Ten Athletic department at Rutgers University.

I founded an e-commerce startup focused on offering high quality and affordable home decorations through multiple channels. It's not a disruptive approach, but we leverage web analytics along with word-of-mouth of our reputation to grow steadily and organically.

I was pursing a Master in Quantitative Finance and I'm very familiar with finance and financial data. I also have 6 years of IT experience in telecommunication system architecture, project management, and an academic background in Electrical Engineering.
